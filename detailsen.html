Premium Royal Sukkari Dates Package from Sheikh Dates

Sukkari dates are known as one of the rare and premium varieties in markets. Despite growing global and local demand, their cultivation is limited to specific regions. Often called "Golden Dates", they stand out for their unique characteristics, exquisite taste, and numerous health benefits. They offer a moderate sweet flavor, large size, and a beautiful golden-brown color with a soft texture that provides an unforgettable taste experience.

Sheikh Dates presents this luxurious 2kg package of authentic Arabian flavor.

What is Royal Sukkari Dates from Sheikh Dates?

These are premium quality dates with a unique flavor profile, grown on organically nurtured palm trees and hand-harvested to ensure the highest quality. They're not just a distinctive taste but a nutritional powerhouse. Sukkari dates are particularly admired because they contain only fruit sugar and grape sugar, without white sugar. Other date varieties contain white sugar which makes them excessively sweet, and overconsumption can lead to obesity.

Nutritional Benefits of Sukkari Dates from Sheikh Dates

This variety is especially notable for its wonderful taste and high quality. Using Sukkari dates as a dietary supplement provides the body with essential nutrients. They're rich in fiber and vitamin C, and help promote cardiovascular health and strengthen bones thanks to their magnesium, potassium, calcium and other important elements.
